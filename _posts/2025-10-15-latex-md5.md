How to add MD5 checksum of a tex.file into itself?
Here is the code for the primitive `\pdfmdfivesum` option:
```latex
%%%
%%% MD5 number
%%%

\makeatletter
\ifx\pdfmdfivesum\undefined
  \let\pdfmdfivesum\mdfivesum
\fi
\edef\filesum{\pdfmdfivesum file {\jobname}}
\makeatother

\filesum

```
