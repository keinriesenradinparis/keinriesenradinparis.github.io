## Transport

La petite ceinture :
- [page](https://petiteceinture.org) officielle

Paris metro chart :
- [page](https://cartometro.com/cartes/) web
- This map (in high resolution) shows the accurate position of Parisian metro rails, including position of platforms and exits.
- One can also find information on many other European cities.

Galerie of (metro/train) stations and interchange passage:
- [page](http://estacions.albertguillaumes.cat) web in Catalan
- You can choose a city and station from the map to see the 3D map of that station.
- This also covers lots of European cities and some North American cities (+ São Paulo + Buenos Aires)
